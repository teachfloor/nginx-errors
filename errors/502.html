502 Bad Gateway

The web server is returning an unexpected networking error for .

What happened?

A 502 error indicates that the platform encountered a communication issue between internal systems while trying to process your request.

You can think of it like asking a question in class, but the message didn’t reach the teacher correctly — so they couldn’t respond.

This type of issue is usually temporary and resolves on its own. Still, it’s a useful reminder that, much like in a classroom, effective communication is essential — even for digital systems.
